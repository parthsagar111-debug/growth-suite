Committed experiment hypothesis

"Sending a day-28 'reorder in 30 seconds' WhatsApp nudge, no discount, to at-risk customers will lift M2 repeat-purchase rate."

Statistical parameters

Sample / arm1,240
Est. duration3.9 days
Confidence / power95% / 80%
Power curve — sample size vs. minimum detectable effect

Why 1,240/arm: a 3pp MDE needs it — chasing a 1pp effect would need ~9x the sample.

Risk & rationale

Small, fast test — the binding constraint is opt-out risk, not statistical power.

Primary risk is message fatigue — this is the third WhatsApp touch in 28 days for this cohort.

Historical precedent

A comparable code-free nudge for this brand shipped with a 3.4pp lift and negligible opt-out impact — a good prior that this pattern works here.
